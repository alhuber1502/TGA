Bibliography

This selected bibliography of printed materials focuses on studies directly related to Gray and his works. The items currently listed are availbale organised by form of publication (six pages), sorted by year of publication (single page), and sorted alphabetically by author/editor (single page). Readers can also consult the list of links to related online resources for more information on relevant online materials.

  • Editions of Gray's poetry: post-18th century editions (including translations) of the works
  • Editions of Gray's other writings: post-18th century editions of his correspondence, journal, and other prose
  • Bibliographies, biographies, and concordance: bio-bibliographical materials
  • Other media and arts: spoken recordings, music, and works of art
  • Monographs and collections: full-length studies and edited collections
  • Articles: journal articles and book chapters

Gray's circle

  • Thomas Ashton: Portrait (1763) by Sir Joshua Reynolds (1723-1792)
  • Richard Bentley: Portrait by John Giles Eccardt (d. 1779) - in the National Portrait Gallery, London
  • Charles Victor de Bonstetten: Portrait of the sitter as Hamlet, artist unknown - in the possession of M. Wolfgang de Reding-Biberegg
  • John Chute: From a painting (1758) by Gabriel Mathias (1719-1804) - in the Further Drawing Room at the Vyne, The National Trust
  • John Chute: Portrait by Johann Heinrich Müntz (1727-1798) - The Paul Mellon Centre for Studies in British Art, London
  • William Cole: From an engraving by Georg Siegmund Facius (c. 1750-1814) - after a painting by Thomas Kerrich (1748-1828)
  • William Mason: Detail of a portrait by Sir Joshua Reynolds (1723-1792)
  • William Mason: Silhouette of the sitter copying the Venus de Medici by Francis Mapletoft - Courtauld Institute of Art
  • William Mason: Portrait of the sitter holding a copy of the epitaph on his wife - from a painting possibly by Peter Falconet, in the possession of Viscount Harcourt
  • Henrietta Jane Speed: Portrait by Peter Falconet, in the possession of Dr Brian Rhodes - the painting obviously depicts her as she looked later in her life as Comtesse de Viry, many years after her first meeting with Gray in 1750 when she was 22
  • Richard Stonhewer: Portrait (1775) by Sir Joshua Reynolds (1723-1792) - in the possession of the Duke of Grafton
  • Horace Walpole: Painting of the sitter aged 10 (1727-28) by William Hogarth (1697-1764) - The Bridgeman Art Library
  • Horace Walpole: Portrait by John Giles Eccardt (d. 1779) - in the National Portrait Gallery, London
  • Horace Walpole: Portrait by Sir Joshua Reynolds (1723-1792) - in the National Portrait Gallery, London
  • Horace Walpole: Sitter in the library at Strawberry Hill, by Johann Heinrich Müntz (1727-1798) - from the water-colour drawing, 1756, in the possession of Com. Colin Campbell-Johnston (1960)
  • Horace Walpole: Painting of the sitter (c. 1741) by Rosalba Giovanni Carriera (1675-1757) - The Bridgeman Art Library
  • Horace Walpole: From a pastel by Rosalba Giovanni Carriera (1675-1757) - in the possession of Lord Walpole
  • Horace Walpole: From a painting (c. 1734) by Jonathan Richardson (1667-1745) - in the possession of the Earl of Waldegrave

<paratext> Extempore by Mr. Gr[ay]. on Dr. K[eene]. B[ishop]. of C[hester].
<paratext>
The Bishop of Chester
Though wiser than Nestor
And fairer than Esther,
If you scratch him will fester.
<paratext>
<paratext> One day the Bishop having offered to give a Gentleman a Goose,
<paratext> Mr. Gr[ay]. composed his Epitaph, thus.
<paratext>
Here lies Edmund Keene Lord Bishop of Chester,
He eat a fat goose and could not digest her&mdash;
<paratext>
<paratext> And this upon his Lady&mdash;
<paratext>
Here lies Mrs Keene the Bishop of Chester,
She had a bad face which did sadly molest her.
